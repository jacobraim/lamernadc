Our Story

A Table for the Neighborhood

La Merna is a Chicago-Italian restaurant and tavern nestled in the heart of Dupont Circle. We believe dinner should feel like coming home — unhurried, warm, and full of something worth savoring.

Our kitchen draws from the rich traditions of Italian-American cooking as it evolved in Chicago — handmade pastas, bold red sauces, creative seasonal plates — paired with an honest bar program that pours from dusk to midnight.
